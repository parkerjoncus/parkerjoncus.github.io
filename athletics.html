While studying at Illinois Tech, I have also had the pleasure of playing on the Men's basketball team. I have been on the team and a starter all four years that I have been at IIT. My first year we were rebuilding, so we had a lot of young guys and were not able to do as well as we hoped. In the off season, we all worked hard and were able to turn the program around and go 21-5 while making it to the championship game of the USCAA National Tournament. My junior year we had another successful season of 19-6 and made another appearance at the USCAA National Tournament, but unfornately lost in the first round. This year is my last year on the team and we are officially NCAA Division 3. We are in the Northern Athletics Collegiate Coference and are hoping to win the conference and do well in the NCAA D3 National Tournament. I have been one of the captains of the team my junior and senior year. You can find my profile and statistics here. I am also pretty active outside of basketball and enjoy water skiing, snow skiing, hiking, and all other sports.
